SIGNUP PROCESS

This championship is a multi-split championship with three categories. Gold, Silver and Bronze. Sign up for the category that you think fits you the best. If you are not sure about your split you can select the I am not sure category and our team will help you. determinate the best suiting split for you.

Each category has 20 seats. If you raced in The Midweek League or The Winter Challenge you can use the split you were put in to determinate which category you should signup for.

The drivers that finished in the top 5 split of the Silver drivers in MWL2  and TWC will be promoted to Gold.

If you want to signup but your split is full. Please signup as reserve in that category and reach our the the LSR staff on discord. They will try to help you.

CAR CHANGES

If you are unsure of what car you want to drive in you can change your car 72 hours prior to start of the first race. When the championship has started you will race the car for the full championship - no car changes will be allowed

JOIN THE DISCORD

You will also have to join the LSR discord

CANT MAKE A RACE?

Life happens and we can't all make all of the races. If you cant make a race you will have to "withdraw" from the race. If you do not do this you will receive penalty points. These penalty points will be deducted from your overall points in the championship. You are allowed to withdraw from 6 races. It is not enough to tell on discord that you can't make a race

CUSTOM LIVERIES

The use of custom liveries made outside of the game is permitted in this event. However, the use of these liveries is not officially supported by the game developers. For syncing liveries we will be using awesome-simracing. Please make sure that you have setup the client and that your api-key is up to date. Read how it works
